    About Me

    My name is Edmark C. Talingting, and I come from Apas, Cebu City. Growing up as the third child in a family of six, I’ve learned the importance of hard work, resilience, and staying grounded. While people often describe me as kind and hardworking, I sometimes feel awkward because, like everyone, I have my moments of self-doubt. Despite this, I always strive to push forward and be better each day.

    I’ve always been passionate about technology. I see it as the future and a gateway to solving the world’s most pressing challenges. There’s something incredibly satisfying about seeing innovative tech creations that shape our world, and that inspires me to build something of my own one day. This love for technology is what drives me to pursue my degree in Information Technology, which I consider the foundation for my dreams of becoming a tech innovator.

    My ultimate goal is to become a software developer, where I can turn my ideas into innovative software that helps people and businesses alike. For me, software development is not just about writing code—it’s about creating solutions that align with my vision of a more advanced and efficient world. I am excited to continue on this journey, knowing that every step brings me closer to contributing to the future of technology.
